Hoshi Sweep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: JUNE 12, 2026

The short version

Hoshi Sweep collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connections. Everything you do stays on your device.

What we store

Only your in-game progress (levels completed and stars earned) and your sound and vibration preferences, saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes it all.

Children

Hoshi Sweep is suitable for everyone and complies with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the app's listing page on the store.
